Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Flux Node Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All sensitive information about your Flux Nodes (such as IP addresses or collateral details) is processed locally on your device and never leaves it.

The only data that may be collected is fully anonymous feature usage analytics and general device information (such as OS version, app version, or screen resolution). This information is used solely to improve the app and cannot be used to identify you personally.

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future as Flux Node Watcher gains new features. By continuing to use the app after any changes to this Privacy Policy, you agree to the updated terms.
